DESCRIPTION

Smell fantastic. Anthony 1 is enticing, intriguing, and irresistibly fresh and smooth.The first fine fragrance from Anthony. Push the atmosphere even greener with lemon, lavender, pine and smoldering sandalwood.

The opening is expansive and recalls bright, fresh air with the zest of first-season harvested Calabrian lemon, flanked by a lightly spicy accord of pink peppercorn and Guatemalan cardamom. Bridging into the heart of French lavender suggests a cooling smoothness while pine and Haitian vetiver push the atmosphere even greener. Timbersilk, an ultra-modern abstract woody element, forms a structure with pure Moroccan cedarwood absolute and Australian sandalwood fir absolue, creating a luxe wood tone so detailed it's almost tactile. A rare note of Indian cypriol gives the fragrance a unique leathery quality. At the base, ambrox, amber and musks provide a smoldering powder that diffuses throughout.

  • • Top: Calabrian lemon primofiore, pink peppercorn, Guatemalan cardamom.
  • • Middle: French lavender, Haitian vetiver, pine, timbersilk.
  • • Base: Ambrox, Australian sandalwood fir absolue, Moroccan cedarwood atlas, amber, musks, cypriol.
