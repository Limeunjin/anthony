For pre-shaving, rub a small amount of oil into palms, massage into face and follow with Anthony Shave Cream or Shave Gel. For beard grooming, apply a small amount of oil into palms , rub together and distribute evenly into your beard. Be sure to massage into skin as well. Use as needed.
